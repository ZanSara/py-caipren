How to reach the hut

1. How to reach the starting point in Chiareggio

When you reach the city of Sondrio, turn north following the indication for Chiesa in Valmalenco. Once you have passed the city of Chiesa, you continue to village of San Giuseppe, after which you will continue along a narrow road to reach Chiareggio. Beyond the village you can find a good and ample parking on the side of the Mallero stream.

Click here to see the precise location of the parking on Google Maps or on OpenStreetMaps.

2. The trails

Once you reached the starting points, there are several trails you can follow:

  • "Alpi Vazzeda" trail 301-305 (get the gpx)
    The classic trail to reach the hut. Follows the path for the 3rd stop of Valmalenco's "Alta Via"

  • "Alta Via" trail through Val Sissone AV301-305 (get the gpx)
    This trail passes in front of the Disgrazia glacier. Very beautiful scenery, especially from the hut down to the valley.

  • "Alpe Sissone" trail 325 (get the gpx)
    The shortest trail to reach the hut. Includes some very steep sections.

3. Circular trails and other trails

Despite its apparently remote location, the hut is part of Valmalenco's Alta Via and it's the starting point of direct trails to other huts in the area.

  • Circular trail to Forni Hut (Switzerland) (get the gpx)
    Recently opened and still unofficial, goes to Forni Hut (in Switzerland) through the Forno Pass and returns through the Vazzeda Pass. Suitable for properly equipped, expert hikers only. PDF available in Italian only (WIP).

  • Trail to Longoni Hut through Alpe dell'Oro (get the gpx)
    If you are already following the "Alta Via" route and you are heading for Rif. Longoni from our hut, this is the best path without go down to Chiareggio. PDF available in Italian only (WIP).

  • "Sentiero Italia" stop 291B (get the gpx)
    Gerli-Porro Hut (1960m); Forbicina (1620m); Del Grande Camerini Hut (2580m); Chiareggio (1610m). PDF available in Italian only (WIP).
