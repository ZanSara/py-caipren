The Hut Del Grande - Remo Camerini

THE HUT WILL OPEN ON 27/6

Overnight stay is not possible. The winter room is closed. Takeaway lunch only (booking is recommended).

The hut Mario Del Grande - Remo Camerini is located at 2580m, on the water line between Val Sissone and Val Bona in Valmalenco, close to Chiareggio (SO).

The hut is located on a magnificent spot with clear view over the north side of Mt. Disgrazia. Is owned by the Italian Alpine Club's branch of Sovico (MB), and is managed during the summer only by volunteers that alternate every week.

Check out the menu for more information on how to reach us, or visit our social profiles on Facebook and Instagram. We're even on Tripadvisor! In any case, the best way to contact us is to write to our email address. During the opening season, you can also reach us by phone at 0342 556010.
